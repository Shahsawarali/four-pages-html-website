Introduction:

Nestled in the heart of Asia Gate, Peshawar, Sarhad Cafe is a sanctuary for tea and coffee aficionados. Our carefully curated menu boasts an array of exquisite teas and coffees sourced from across Asia. From the robust kick of a freshly brewed espresso to the delicate floral notes of a Darjeeling white tea, we offer a sensory journey through the diverse landscapes of flavor. Indulge in the earthy richness of Assam black tea, or savor the nuanced profiles of oolong and green teas. For coffee lovers, our baristas craft each cup with precision and passion, ensuring a perfect brew every time. From single-origin beans to decadent blends, our coffee offerings are a testament to the artistry of the brew. Join us at Sarhad Cafe, where every sip is an exploration of taste, and every cup tells a tale of the ancient trading routes. Find us at Asia Gate, Peshawar, and embark on a journey through the world of teas and coffees like never before.
